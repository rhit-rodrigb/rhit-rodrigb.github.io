About the Author

So a little about myself, I, like many others, got interested in coding by playing games. I was fascinated at how the systems in games worked, from keeping track of player stats in single player games to how a game knows when two objects make contact. As I got older, I started to wonder how many other systems worked like bank systems keeping track of everyones finances, traffic lights knowing when to turn red, yellow, or green, and how user information is tracked on a website to name a few. Now, I have become intrigued about how AI systems work. I see AI as something that is going to stick around for a long time, even if the AI bubble bursts. As such, I believe it is important to understand how they work and how they can be integrated into my workflow.
